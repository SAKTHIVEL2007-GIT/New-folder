THE GREATEST OF ALLTIME

**Lionel Messi** is an Argentine footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players in history, known for his extraordinary dribbling, vision, and goal-scoring ability. He rose to legendary status at FC Barcelona, won a record eight Ballon d’Or awards, and crowned his career by leading Argentina to victory at the 2022 FIFA World Cup, before continuing his impact with Inter Miami.

BEST PLAYEREVER IN HISTORY

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese footballer considered one of the greatest players of all time, renowned for his athleticism, goal-scoring consistency, and longevity. He has starred for clubs like Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus, won five Ballon d’Or awards, and led Portugal to major international success, including the 2016 UEFA European Championship.
